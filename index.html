| ENG | The Blocky Bikes mod is a large-scale addition to Minecraft (version 1.20.1, Forge), which focuses on realistic and detailed motorcycles, mainly of Soviet and modern production. Here is a detailed description of what exactly this mod adds: 1. Huge fleet of motorcycles The mod contains almost 30 more motorcycle models. Among them you can find: Classics of the USSR: Izh Planeta 3, Izh Planeta 4, Izh Jupiter, Izh Planeta Sport, Izh K-16, Minsk, MT-10 "Dnipro". Legendary Jawa: JAWA 638 models and other variations. Modern sportbikes: Kawasaki Ninja H2R, BMW S1000 RR. Road and other models: Pannonia, Suzuki GS 500, Honda CB 500. 2. Realistic mechanics and crafting The mod does not just add models, but introduces a system of assembly and maintenance: Complex assembly: To get a motorcycle, you need to assemble the engine separately for each model. Spare parts: Specific parts are required for engine crafting: crankcase, connecting rod, piston and a set of tools. Fuel system: Gasoline (95th) and gas stations for refueling equipment appear in the game. 3. Additional items (Equipment and equipment) In addition to the bikes themselves, the mod adds: Helmets: Various types of protective equipment for the player. Canisters: For carrying fuel. Special blocks: For example, an assembly table or decorative elements related to the garage theme. 4. Technical Features Detailing: The models have high-quality textures and a sophisticated appearance, which distinguishes them from standard "block" models. Version: The current version is being developed for Minecraft 1.20.1 based on Forge. This mod is ideal for those who want to create their own bike collection, open a motorcycle workshop, or just quickly travel the game world with style. |
